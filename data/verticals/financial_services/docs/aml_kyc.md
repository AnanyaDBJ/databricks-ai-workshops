# Anti-Money Laundering and KYC Policy

Meridian Capital Partners maintains a risk-based AML program aligned with applicable regulations. All clients must complete Know Your Customer (KYC) verification before account activation.

## Customer Due Diligence

- **Standard due diligence** for retail and high-net-worth individual accounts
- **Enhanced due diligence (EDD)** for politically exposed persons (PEPs), offshore entities, and accounts above $5M AUM
- Re-verification every 24 months, or upon material change in client profile

## Monitoring

Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) must be filed within 30 calendar days of detection. Transaction monitoring thresholds:

| Alert Type | Threshold |
|------------|-----------|
| Large cash movement | $10,000 single day |
| Rapid in/out pattern | 3+ round trips within 5 business days |
| High-risk jurisdiction | Any wire to/from FATF grey-list country |

## Record Retention

KYC documentation and transaction records are retained for seven years from account closure.
