# Best Execution Policy

Meridian Capital Partners is committed to obtaining the most favorable terms reasonably available for client orders.

## Order Routing

Equity orders are routed to registered exchanges, ATSs, and market makers based on:

1. Price improvement opportunity
2. Probability of execution
3. Speed of execution
4. Total transaction cost including rebates and fees

## Review

Quarterly best-ex scorecards are produced by the Trading Compliance team. Material changes to routing logic require prior approval from the CCO.

## Client Disclosure

Upon request, clients may receive a routing and execution summary for the prior 12 months.
