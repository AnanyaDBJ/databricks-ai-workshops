# Market Conduct Policy

All employees and associated persons must comply with market integrity rules.

## Prohibited Conduct

- Front-running client orders
- Trading ahead of research publication without compliance pre-clearance
- Sharing material non-public information (MNPI)
- Spoofing, layering, or manipulative quoting

## Personal Trading

Employees must pre-clear personal trades in covered securities. Blackout periods apply around IPO allocations and sensitive corporate actions.

## Gifts and Entertainment

Gifts to clients or brokers exceeding $100 per person per year require compliance registration.
