# Client Suitability Policy

Recommendations and discretionary strategies must be suitable based on the client's investment profile on file.

## Required Profile Elements

- Investment objective (preservation, income, growth, speculation)
- Time horizon
- Risk tolerance score (conservative, moderate, aggressive)
- Liquidity needs
- Options approval level (0–4)

## Restricted Products

Structured products and leveraged ETFs require explicit opt-in and supervisor approval for clients with conservative or moderate risk ratings.

## Suitability Reviews

Annual review for discretionary accounts; triggered review within 10 days of material life event or complaint.
