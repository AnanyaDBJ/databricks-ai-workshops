# Settlement and Corporate Actions Policy

## Standard Settlement

- US equities: T+1
- US listed options: T+1
- Fixed income: T+1 to T+3 by instrument

## Failed Trades

Fails must be aged and resolved within 30 calendar days. Buy-in procedures apply after CNS notice for equities.

## Corporate Actions

Clients receive proactive notification of voluntary actions (tenders, rights offerings) at least 5 business days before deadline. Mandatory actions (splits, mergers) are processed per Depository Trust Company (DTC) instructions.

## Dividends and Income

Qualified dividend treatment is reported on Form 1099-DIV. Foreign withholding is reclaimed where treaty benefits apply.
