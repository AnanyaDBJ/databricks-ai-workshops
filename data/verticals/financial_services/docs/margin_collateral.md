# Margin and Collateral Policy

This policy governs extension of credit for securities trading at Meridian Capital Partners.

## Initial and Maintenance Margin

| Account Type | Initial Margin | Maintenance Margin |
|--------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Standard brokerage | 50% | 25% |
| Portfolio margin (approved) | 15% | 10% |
| Options — equity | 25% / strategy-based | 25% |

## Margin Calls

Clients receive a margin call when equity falls below maintenance requirements. Cure period:

- **Intraday call:** Funds due by 4:00 PM ET same day
- **Reg T call:** Due within 3 business days
- Failure to meet call may result in liquidation per the Liquidation Procedures section

## Collateral Haircuts

Treasury securities: 2–5% haircut. Corporate bonds: 10–25% by rating. Concentrated equity positions above 30% of portfolio may receive additional 15% haircut.
