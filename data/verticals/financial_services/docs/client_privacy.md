# Client Privacy Policy

Meridian Capital Partners protects non-public personal information in accordance with Regulation S-P and GDPR where applicable.

## Information Collected

- Identity and contact data
- Financial statements and tax documentation
- Trading history and communications
- Authentication and device fingerprints for digital channels

## Sharing

We do not sell client data. Service providers (custody, KYC vendors, market data) receive data under contractual confidentiality obligations only.

## Client Rights

Clients may request access, correction, or deletion subject to regulatory retention requirements. Contact privacy@meridiancapital.com.
